We have been making music since 2007, the main occupation is in production, playing acoustic\ virtual instruments and singing

Ofir Amar

Co Manager

Manager and Founder of O.A Music, main producer and sound engineer. Started as a drummer when he was 10. Then when he was 13 he started playing classical and electric guitar. At the age of 17 he started writing and composing his own original music. In 2020, Ofir began studying at BPM College in a profession called "Singer-Creator" and added another course of sound and production to his studies. Today he specializes in music production in Cubase and Ableton live digital audio workstation, producing his own songs and others people songs
